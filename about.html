About Me

Geographer | Remote Sensing Specialist | GIS Analyst

Introduction

Hi, I’m Dryver Zain Finch, a geographer specializing in remote sensing applications for land and resource monitoring, conservation, and ecological restoration. My work integrates satellite- and UAV-based multispectral imaging with machine learning techniques to support sustainable management of natural resources and hydrological systems. I’m passionate about leveraging geospatial technologies to address environmental challenges and promote accessibility to advanced analytical tools.
