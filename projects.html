Research Projects

Here are some of the research projects I’ve worked on:

Hydrological UAV-based Remote Sensing

Investigated riparian systems using UAV-based LiDAR and multispectral imaging to support ecological restoration and dam impact studies at Cherokee National Forest.

  • Learn More

Spatial Dynamics of Bacillus Anthracis

Analyzed the spatial dynamics of Bacillus anthracis lineages in West Texas to understand pathogen distribution in ranching communities.

  • Learn More

Quantified changes in phenological timing in Appalachian regions using cloud computing methods and regression analysis in R Studio.
